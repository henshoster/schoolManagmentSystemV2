Introduction:

The school is a service that enables the management of students and courses for a school, with different permissions for different types of adminstrators:

  • Owner - only 1 in the system, Full access to all services.
  • Manager - Full access to all services excluding owner details(view and edit) and self role editing / removing option.
  • Sales - No access to administrators tab, not allowed to create/remove/edit courses.

Getting started:
  1. Required PHP + MYSQL.
  2. Clone or Download the files from here.
  3. Open database/database.php file and change 4 CONST variables (lines 4-7): HOST, USERNAME, PASSWORD, DATABASE_NAME for details of your MYSQL server.
    Please make sure your DATABASE_NAME is not already taken.
  4. Click Login in the top-right corner or here.
  5. First Login as the Owner (Owner details can be modified under the administrators tab):
    Email: admin@theschool.com
    Password: admin
  6. Insert your school main page template to 'mainpage/default_main_page_tpl.php', feel free to modify and delete all files under this folder, This folder is separated from the app.
  7. Good luck :-)
